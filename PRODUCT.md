# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Manufacturing quality inspectors and technicians working on industrial production lines for coating and welding. They monitor live camera feeds, approve object crops, review defect segmentation results, and export audit-ready reports during production work.

## Product Purpose

MQC-AI automates manufacturing quality control with computer vision. Success means inspectors can move from live object detection or uploaded media to reviewed QC batches with fewer manual steps, clear model context, reliable crop approval, and traceable audit output.

## Brand Personality

Precise, industrial, dependable. The interface should feel like production equipment software: direct, legible, restrained, and focused on repeatable inspection work.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style dashboards, decorative gradients, soft shadows, rounded cards, glass effects, playful motion, and experimental controls that slow down operators. Avoid hiding operational state or model context behind cosmetic UI.

## Design Principles

- Keep inspection actions explicit and reversible.
- Make model, confidence, source, status, and progress visible near the work.
- Prefer dense, scannable production UI over decorative composition.
- Use consistent Carbon-style controls across monitoring, settings, review, and reporting.
- Treat empty, loading, error, and no-model states as core workflow states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable IBM Plex typography, sufficient contrast, keyboard-focus visibility, reduced-motion alternatives, and semantic status color with text labels so operators can work across light and dark modes without relying on color alone.
